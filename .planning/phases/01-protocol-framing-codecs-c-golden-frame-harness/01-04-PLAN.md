State flags: 0x0004 request, 0x0005 response.
Command IDs: 0x01 ReadDeviceInfo, 0x02 Read, 0x03 Write, 0x04 ReadState, 0x05 WriteControl,
             0x06 AddDeviceNotification, 0x07 DeleteDeviceNotification, 0x08 DeviceNotification, 0x09 ReadWrite.
