<objective>
Add the `localAddress` seam to the transport layer so the Phase-4 AmsRouter can
auto-derive the source AmsNetId as `<ip>.1.1` from the socket's local IPv4
(AdsLib parity). This is the single genuinely-new Dart concern the research
identified: `SocketTransport` currently exposes only connect/add/inbound/close
and has no way to report the local address obtained via `getsockname`.

Purpose: unblock ROUTE-03 source-NetId auto-derivation without touching the
connection or router layers yet.
Output: `AdsTransport.localAddress`, its `SocketTransport` and `FakeTransport`
implementations, and their tests.
</objective>
